Best Time to Book Umrah Flights

When to book, which months are cheapest, and how to find the best fares for your Umrah journey from the UK and worldwide.

Why Timing Matters

Umrah can be performed at any time of year, which means flight prices vary significantly depending on when you travel. Unlike Hajj, you have real flexibility — and that flexibility can save you hundreds of pounds. Understanding when demand is high and when airlines drop prices is the key to finding a good deal.

💡 Best Value Window

January, February and September–November offer the best combination of lower prices and cooler temperatures in Makkah. Avoid Ramadan and UK school holidays if price is your priority.

How Far in Advance to Book

For regular Umrah travel, booking 6–10 weeks ahead gives the best balance of price and availability. For Ramadan travel, book 3–4 months in advance — prices and seats move quickly once demand picks up.

Best Airlines for Umrah Flights

  • Saudi Arabian Airlines (Saudia) — direct from London Heathrow to Jeddah and Madinah
  • flynas — budget Saudi carrier, competitive fares
  • Emirates — via Dubai, excellent from regional UK airports
  • Qatar Airways — via Doha, highly rated service
  • Turkish Airlines — via Istanbul, often cheapest from UK regional airports

Quick Money-Saving Tips

  • Search on Tuesday or Wednesday — fares can be lower mid-week
  • Check regional airports — Manchester, Birmingham and Glasgow often beat London prices
  • Consider connecting flights — they add time but can save £100–£200 per person
  • Try an open-jaw route — fly into Madinah, out of Jeddah (or vice versa)
  • Compare multiple platforms before booking
